Senior AI Research Scientist

Company: Innovative AI Solutions Inc.
Location: Remote / Hybrid

Job Overview:
We're looking for a Senior AI Research Scientist to lead cutting-edge research in artificial intelligence and machine learning. The ideal candidate will have a strong background in deep learning, reinforcement learning, and natural language processing, with publications in top-tier conferences or journals.

Key Responsibilities:
- Conduct original research in AI/ML with a focus on transformer architectures and large language models
- Design and implement novel neural network architectures for solving complex problems
- Lead research projects and mentor junior team members
- Collaborate with engineering teams to transition research into production systems
- Publish research findings in top conferences (NeurIPS, ICML, ICLR, ACL)
- Stay current with latest advancements in AI research and identify new opportunities

Requirements:
- PhD in Computer Science, Machine Learning, or related field (or equivalent research experience)
- 5+ years of experience in AI/ML research
- Strong publication record in top-tier ML/AI conferences or journals
- Expert-level knowledge of PyTorch or TensorFlow
- Experience with large language models and attention mechanisms
- Proficiency in Python and data processing libraries
- Excellent mathematical foundation in statistics, linear algebra, and calculus
- Ability to communicate complex technical concepts clearly

Preferred Qualifications:
- Experience with computer vision and multimodal learning
- Familiarity with distributed computing and GPU optimization
- Prior experience leading research teams
- Open-source contributions to major ML frameworks or libraries
